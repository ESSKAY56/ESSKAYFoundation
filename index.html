In Loving Memory of

Suresh Kumar Sharma

Forward, Not Backward. Hope, Not Surrender.

The Father Who Taught Us How to Stand

Some fathers protect you with their hands. Some protect you with their words. And a rare few protect you with their mindset — the way they teach you how to face life itself.

He was that kind of father.

He didn’t just raise us—he steadied us. In moments when we were anxious, he was calm. When we were unsure, he was clear. When life felt heavy, he somehow made it feel carryable. His presence alone gave us courage—not because he promised easy outcomes, but because he showed us how to stand strong in difficult times.

Even today, when we face something hard, our first instinct is still the same: What would he say right now? And the answer is always — look ahead, not back.

Reliable Strength

In every family there is someone whose steadiness becomes everyone else’s confidence. For us, it was him. We measured the seriousness of a situation by his reaction. If he was composed, we felt safe. If he said, “We’ll handle it,” the matter already felt half solved. He stood in front of problems and worked through them step by step.

Forward Thinking

He refused to let setbacks become identity. He acknowledged pain, but he did not surrender perspective. Regret, blame, and “what if” conversations never lasted long around him. He would gently move the conversation toward action and next steps. His hope was disciplined thinking.

When kidney disease entered his life, it changed his physical comfort — but it did not change his character. Dialysis routines were demanding and fatigue was real, yet he stayed mentally forward-looking. He showed us that dignity is not about circumstances; it is about the posture of the spirit.

"Even in his last days, when his body was failing, his mind was still pointing forward. That's when we truly understood: his nature wasn't just personality. It was conviction. It was choice."

"Forward, not backward. Action, not self-pity. Hope, not surrender."

The ESSKAY Spirit

His Legacy in Motion

Our Mission

To provide life-saving dialysis support to economically disadvantaged kidney patients across India. We believe kidney disease should not be a death sentence because of financial hardship.

Our Continuity

A life like his should continue creating impact. This work is not charity alone — it is continuity. A way of extending his values into the lives of people who need strength and support today.
